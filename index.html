So, what's the plan?

Oh boy, let us tell you, it's not easy to find a place for 70 people in the middle of Copenhagen! Time was ticking fast a month or two ago, and we were starting to feel a liiiitle bit stressed. As luck would have it, we decided to go on a little bike ride in the area close to where we live - and lo and behold, we stumbled upon a gem of a café, tucked perfectly in between nature and the city.

Traktørstedet Vestamager (Granatvej 9, 2770 Kastrup), will be the official location for our wedding! Here there will be room to eat delicious and simple food made from local products, have a few drinks, and dance the night away.
But hey, what about a cermony and all that jazz? Fear not! We're working hard to figure out the details, but we know for certain that our ceremony will be close by, in nature, and will be held before meeting for dinner at Traktørstedet. The rest is a suprise!
